How to use:

  • Paste your JWT token in the input field and click "Connect".
  • Once connected, click "Start Transcription" to begin recording.
  • Speak clearly into your microphone.
  • Watch as your speech is transcribed in real-time.
  • Click "Stop Transcription" when you're done.
  • Click "Save to Heuristic" to submit your transcription for processing.
  • Use "Clear Transcript" to remove all transcribed text.
